A pointer to a TerrainStorageCell
Remarks
The terrain storage can be understood as a mipmap where each level of detail is represented by a mipmap level. This means y is the coordinate in z direction of the terrain. Also x and y aren't world space coordinates but are the coordinates of a specific terrain node in their level of detail. E.g at a level of detail of 5 and a rootNodeCount of 9 x and y are in range (0, 2^(5-1) * 9)
